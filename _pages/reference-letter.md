If you would like to request a recommendation letter, please read the guidelines below and contact me by email.

Timeline
======
* Please request at least 4 weeks before the earliest deadline.

Materials
======
* CV or resume
* Unofficial transcript
* Draft statement of purpose or cover letter (if applicable)
* List of programs or opportunities with deadlines and submission instructions

Eligibility
======
* I can write letters for students I have taught, advised, or supervised.

Notes
======
* Please include a brief summary of our interaction and your goals.
* I do not accept gifts or honoraria for recommendation letters.
